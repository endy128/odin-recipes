Beans on Toast

Description

If you're from Britain, you're probably wondering why on earth there's even a need for a “recipe” for beans on toast, am I right?

However, if you see what is out there on Google when you search “beans on toast”, you too will be horrified! Recipes from people who I'm guessing have never even stepped foot in the UK. And so much misinformation about what it is, that the steam was coming out of my ears! I felt like I had to write about beans on toast asap! So here were are.

Ingredients

  • 1 tin of Heinz baked beans
  • 2 Slices of fresh bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Empty the tin into a saucepan and gently heat, do not boil
  2. Toast two slices of bread until golden
  3. Butter the toast
  4. Pour the delicious beans over the freshly buttered toast
  5. ENJOY!
